Who are we?

JOSH, the alumni organization of St.James is a steadily growing community of St.James alumni from the first batch of 1993 to the current batch of 2014. The members range from college freshers to men and women in positions of responsibility around the world. Formed in 2010 with around 150 members, JOSH has grown gratitude for the alma mater keeps us going.

What do we do?

The common goals of every alumni in the community have been realised through the following ventures.
1. La’ Orientation Professionelle – Career Counselling Program
Getting exposed to various options in the future stream is the best thing a student can get. Information on what to do next is the prime target of the Career Counselling Program See More..
2. JAMFEST – The inter- school cultural, literary fest
JAMFEST is the celebration of young talents. It is an inter-school cultural, literary fest which happens for two days, once a year. Various schools in and around Trichy participates in this event to make a wonderful expo of their student’s skills See More..
3. JOSH Talent League- The intra school talent hunt
It is an intra-school talent hunt conducted exclusively for the students of St James Matric Hr Sec School. It happens for a day, once a year See More..
