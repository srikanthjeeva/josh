L’Orientation Professionelle

Helping our juniors to perfectly fit in their passion is exactly what we do here. We do two Career Counselling phases, for 10th students and for 12th students. On this day, the students will be given guidance about what their next step going to be, by the Joshians who are proficient in various fields. So the entire event will be full of career info, motivational talks and fun riddles. L’Orientation Professionelle happens twice a year that is one for 10th students and another for 12th students. We encourage queries from the students after each session so that we get to know what they actually want to know. Information regarding competitive exams, course details, college details, board exam tips, entrance exam details will be provided to the students on this day. So thus this event takes our student’s perception of education to a complete different level.

JAMFEST

JAMFEST embraces twenty five plus events, titled under different categories like arts, literary and cultural. Each and every event is designed for senior and junior category separately. Participants from distinctive schools, around 400 students compete to win the overall final trophy. A judge panel of one internal judge (Joshian who is skilled at that particular event), one external judge, and a staff member of James will be judging the entire event and the winners will be decided by the total points awarded by the judge panel. Food stalls, fun games and many other lively factors will be going on simultaneously. We shaped JAMFEST to create wings to young talents.

JOSH Talent League

JOSH Talent League is a real treat for the students of St James. Students from kinder garden to higher secondary, participates in JTL. JTL has twenty plus events categorised as arts, literary and cultural. With the best set of contestants, audiences, judges and staffs we take this event forward to create opportunity and space to showcase the ability of Jameians and to award them for what they are really enthusiastic about.

Alumni Get-togethers

JOSH-The Phoenix is an alumni crew with nearly 1300 members from various parts of the world. We take pleasure in organising events like Career Counselling, JTL , Jamfest. So to make this happen without any shades of flaws, to strengthen communication among all joshians, to enhance the bond between each of us, to give detailed description on what we are carrying on to each and every alumnus, we put ourselves together on a complacent day to sit and have casual discussions to take JOSH to the next level of improvement. We validate feedbacks after each event and try to put it in a right shape. It gives us satisfaction that we give back something worth and useful to the place which carved us so finely to be in the position we are right now. So we are planning out alumni get-together every year to strengthen JOSH.
