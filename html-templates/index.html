Getting exposed to various options available in the UG and PG realm is the best thing a student can get. Information on what to do immediately after high school is the prime target of the Career Counselling Program

JTL is an intra-school talent hunt conducted exclusively for the students of St. James Matric Hr Sec School. It happens for a day, every year.
